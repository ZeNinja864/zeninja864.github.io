The Xeriscape Network, 'Xeriscape', is a mutli-platform gaming community with a focus on automation that I created back in 2010. It started out as a small group of friends who wanted to spend time together playing various games including Minecraft to Team Fortress 2. I’m the founder and owner of the community, and I share ownership with my wife, Raizenith. Over the years, it has helped me grow into a better developer and has taught me many things that I now refer to and use on a daily basis.

I have developed and am actively working on several Discord bots, each serving a unique purpose and showcasing different aspects of my programming journey.
